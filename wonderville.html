01 — The brief

Ice cream sells itself on flavour. Wonderville wanted to sell the story you tell while you eat it.

A tub that reads like a children's book — hand-drawn worlds, hidden characters, and a flavour that's also the plot. Something a kid begs to keep after the last scoop.

02 — The idea

Every tub is a little storybook.

Illustrated scenes wrap all the way around, a golden book sits at the centre of each design, and the lid opens like a cover. The flavour name doubles as a chapter title.
