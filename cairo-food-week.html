One week

For one week the whole city talks about food, across dozens of venues that have nothing else in common.

So the system had to stretch a long way: a poster, a phone screen, a sign hanging over a hall, all obviously the same thing. Friendly rather than corporate, since the whole point is people sitting down together.

Two shapes

The shapes overlap, the way people do.

The graphics are simple food shapes that keep running into each other. Two circles read as a plate, then as two people leaning over one.
