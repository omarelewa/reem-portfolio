Beige

Clean beauty had gone very beige. Mother Naked wanted to be honest about what's inside without whispering it.

The recipes are simple and there's nothing to hide, so the packaging says everything out loud. I wanted a jar you'd leave on the shelf instead of hiding in a cabinet.
