01 — The brief

Lip balm is the most boring shelf in the shop. Ours had to taste like a memory, not a chapstick.

The line needed to feel indulgent and a little French — small enough to slip in a pocket, rich enough to feel like a treat you saved for yourself.

02 — The idea

A balm that behaves like a chocolate bar.

A hand-drawn retro logotype, wrappers that fold like confectionery, and a cocoa-and-cream palette you can almost smell. The tube even snaps shut like a chocolate box.
