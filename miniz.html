The name

Kids outgrow everything, so miniz needed something they'd actually want to keep.

That turned into a cast rather than a logo. mr mini is a crocodile, and he came with five friends who show up across the clothes and the tags.

The cast

mr mini, and everyone he brought with him.

There's a pink tiger, a blue elephant, a giraffe, a lynx, and a cat drawn far longer than any cat needs to be. All hand-drawn, and they turn up on prints and hang-tags so there's usually something to find.
