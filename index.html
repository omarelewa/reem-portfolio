 About

I care more about what a brand is trying to say than how the logo looks.

I'm Reem, a graphic designer and art director from Cairo. I studied finance at the German University in Cairo before switching to graphic design partway through. Since then I've worked at a few Cairo studios — Jude Benhalim, then almost three years at Matter — and today I'm a senior graphic designer at Lub Creative. The projects run from skincare and real estate to kids' clothes, a marathon and a food festival.

The part I like most is the story underneath a brand — why it exists and what it should sound like — and I have a soft spot for Arabic type. I work in Arabic and English, and get by in French and German. Away from design I swim: I've raced as a national and Arab champion, and I hold a Guinness World Record.
