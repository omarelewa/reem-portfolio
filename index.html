 About

I design brands that feel like a person, not a logo.

I'm Reem — a graphic designer and art director from Cairo. I studied finance for two years before I admitted I'd rather spend my life making things than counting them. Since then I've built identities, packaging and campaigns for skincare, real estate, food, fashion and everything in between.

I care most about the story underneath a brand — the reason it exists — and about Arabic type, which I think is some of the most beautiful design in the world. When I'm not designing, I'm probably in a pool: I swim competitively and hold a Guinness World Record.
