01 — The brief

Every developer sells square metres. LARZ had to sell a feeling — a different way of living, not a floor plan.

They needed a mark that felt architectural and modern — and one idea that could scale from a business card to a lit billboard in a metro tunnel.

02 — The idea

Design a window, not a wall.

The mark is a portal — a frame you look through into a new dimension of life. An electric-blue burst marks the moment it opens.

The signature moment

Drag the wall away.
