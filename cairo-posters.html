Why

Nobody asked for this one. I wanted to see how far Arabic letterforms could bend before they stopped being readable.

Cairo is loud and disorganised and somehow completely legible once you've lived in it a while. That contradiction is the whole series.

Bend it

Bend the logic.

Drag from order to chaos and watch القاهرة come apart.
